# Visual Parity Checklist

Primary reference: approved Shree Aashapura Supermarket wireframe HTML.

- Top Header: desktop-only dark information bar with phone, hours, and social/share action preserved.
- Navigation: sticky layout with logo left, links centered on desktop, WhatsApp CTA right, same link hierarchy.
- Hero: two-column layout, large display headline, supporting copy, two CTAs, four trust indicators, large grocery image.
- Quick Stats: four cards in the same sequence and hierarchy.
- About: split layout with image left and story/features right; four feature cards preserved.
- Shop By Category: category-focused grid with circular image area and title, no product-catalog conversion.
- Why Choose Us: six-card grid with icon, heading, and supporting copy.
- Featured Brands: auto-scrolling brand marquee with hover pause and hover color reveal.
- Home Delivery: two-column image/content layout with WhatsApp and call CTAs.
- Location: split card layout with Google Maps embed left and store details right.
- Contact: contact information left, validated frontend form right with success/error states.
- Footer: four-column structure with brand summary, quick links, categories, and contact information.
- Floating WhatsApp: fixed bottom-right CTA using the mandated WhatsApp URL.
